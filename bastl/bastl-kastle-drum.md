# Bastl Kastle Drum

## About

Mini modular digital synthesiser, released in 2020. Price 90 euros. It is a new firmware and face panel for the Bastl Kastle v.15 synthesizer by Bastl Instruments.

## Enclosure

Made of fiberglass.

## Power

3x AA batteries or micro USB jack.

## Front panel

* 7x knobs:
  * DRUM MOD knob
  * PITCH MOD knob
  * TEMPO MOD knob
  * DECAY knob 
  * DRUM knob
  * PITCH knob
  * TEMPO knob

* 1x toggle switch:
  * BATT / USB switch

* Patchbay:
  * 2x I/O: L, R
  * 2x POWER: +, -
  * 2x TRIG IN
  * 3x DRUM MOD
  * 3x PITCH MOD
  * 3x TEMPO MOD
  * 3x DECAY
  * 2x CLK IN
  * 2x FEED PATTERN
  * 2x DRUMS
  * 3x NOISES
  * 3x CLK
  * 3x LFO
  * 3x PATTERN

## Back panel

* 1x USB POWER jack: power with micro USB cable
* 1x I/0 jack: 
* 1x OUT jack: 1/8" stereo TRS output, it can drive headphones or connected to a mixer.
